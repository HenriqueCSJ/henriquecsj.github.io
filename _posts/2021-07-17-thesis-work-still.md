My thesis work is reaching 300 pages and the funny thing is that I
started writing backwards: the last chapters first and now I am finishing with
the introduction. Writing is fun, to be honest, but I have to confess, I am not
a fast writer, I type with four fingers, and I lack the discipline to do it
right. To be honest, I probably should have finished everything at this point, but
I have procrastination moments that put entire days to waste, and I am dealing
with something that feels like a mild depression that comes and goes like moon
phases and, usually, gets me in a more “contemplative” mood about my life and
future.

Today it feels like I am getting sick with something, but I still have
plans to start my take on magnetic anisotropy:

$$
\hat{\mathcal{H}}=D\left(S_z^2-\frac{1}{3}S(S+1)\right)+E(S_x^2-S_y^2)
$$
